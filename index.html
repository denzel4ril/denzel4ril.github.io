About Our School

Established in 1985, Chintheche Primary School has been a cornerstone of education in the Nkhata Bay district of Malawi. We are committed to providing inclusive, quality education for all learners, focusing on academic growth, discipline, and creativity to prepare our pupils for future success.

Our campus features modern classrooms, a library, sports facilities, and computer lab to ensure our students receive a well-rounded education that prepares them for the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing learning environment that empowers students to achieve their full potential academically, socially, and emotionally, while instilling values of integrity, respect, and community service.

Our Vision

To be a leading primary educational institution in Malawi that develops well-rounded, confident, and responsible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential.
