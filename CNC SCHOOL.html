About Us

Our History

Chintheche Primary School was established in 2000 by Malawi Government with a mission to provide quality education to children in the Chintheche area. Over the years, we have grown into a respected institution known for academic excellence and holistic development of learners.

Mission, Vision & Core Values

Mission: To provide quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential and become responsible citizens.

Vision: To be a center of academic excellence that nurtures future leaders and innovators.

Core Values: Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Responsibility, and Community.

    Admissions

    We welcome new students to Chintheche Primary School. The admission process is straightforward and designed to be accessible to all families.

    Admission Requirements

    • Completed application form
    • Birth certificate
    • Previous school records (if transferring)
    • Health immunization records

    Admission Process

    1. Collect an application form from the school office
    2. Submit the completed form with required documents
    3. Attend a brief interview/assessment (if required)
    4. Receive admission decision
    5. Complete registration and pay necessary fees

    For more information about admissions, please contact our administration office during school hours.
